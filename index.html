WIKIX

A practical wiki for teams that ship.

Markdown and MDX at the core, predictable routing, and an editing flow that stays simple as your project grows.

Single wiki per deployment Root-relative URLs Atomic content writes

Why teams delay documentation

  • Wiki setup often becomes a parallel engineering task.
  • Writers and maintainers end up with different workflows.
  • Publishing can hide where the source of truth actually lives.

Wikix keeps the model straightforward

Content

Pages are normal .md and .mdx files in one wiki root.

Routing

Homepage is /, pages are /{slug}, no wiki prefixes.

Control

RBAC, audit trail, and operational sync/reindex paths are built in.

Publish

Atomic write flow keeps deployed docs stable and reviewable.

Who benefits first

Game and mod teams

Ship patch notes, lore, and onboarding pages without overhead.

Smaller open-source projects

Keep architecture and contributor docs close to the code workflow.

Internal tooling teams

Run private docs with clear role boundaries and predictable behavior.

AI-assisted workflows

Markdown-first structure stays machine-friendly and easy to automate later.

Rollout in one sprint

  1. Bootstrap wiki and seed current markdown pages.
  2. Set maintainer/contributor/reader roles.
  3. Capture consistent product screenshots for docs and social posts.

Wikix is focused on being easy to run, easy to reason about, and easy to extend.
